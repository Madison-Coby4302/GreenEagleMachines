We believe it's critical that students be given the opportunity to experience how to grow foods and herbs for culinary purposes as well as medicinal benefits. Hands on experience through traditional gardening, hydroponics, and aquaponics is the best teacher. We plant, care for, and harvest, store, and use our plants so that students can understand their importance and benefits in life. We feel this is a dying practice that needs to be cultivated into a growing trend.
